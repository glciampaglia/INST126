Chapter 1: Mary had a little lamb, whose fleece was white as snow.
Chapter 2: And everywhere that Mary went, the lamb was sure to go.
Chapter 3: It followed her to school one day which was against the rules.
Chapter 4: It made the children laugh and play, to see a lamb at school.
Chapter 5: And so the teacher turned it out, but still it lingered near, And waited patiently about, till Mary did appear.
Chapter 6: "Why does the lamb love Mary so?" the eager children cry.
Chapter 7: "Why, Mary loves the lamb, you know." the teacher did reply.
